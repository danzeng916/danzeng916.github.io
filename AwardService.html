Research Interests
Human Perception
I design methods for face super-resolution, expression recognition and face recognition to facilitate the intelligent human perception, which are useful for applications such as public safety, security, human-computer interaction, and health care.
Animal Perception
I explore UAV tracking, animal face alignment and pose estimation methods to facilitate the intelligent animal perception, which has potential benefits and impact for animal healthcare and wildlife conservation but is largely unexplored.
AIGC for Biometrics
Emerging AI Technologies (e.g., AIGC, LLMs) can be used to revolutionize the perception of Humans and Animals. In particular, combining AIGC with LLMs can reduce the need for labeling and protect the privacy information.
