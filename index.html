About me

Dan Zeng 曾丹, PhD. Research Assistant Professor, Associate Researcher, Postgraduate Supervisor (硕导) Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech).

Dan Zeng is currently a research assistant professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech). She received the B.E. and Ph.D. degrees in computer science and technology from Sichuan University (SCU) in 2013 and 2018, under the supervision of distinguished Prof. Zhisheng You(杰出教授，游志胜教授) and Prof. Qijun Zhao(赵启军教授). Since 2012, she participated in ‘3+2+3’ Joined Study of Bachelor and Doctoral Degree programs at Sichuan University (Top 5%). She worked as a post-doc research fellow in the Data Management and Biometrics (DMB) Lab at the University of Twente (UT), the Netherlands from 2018 to 2020, under the supervision of Prof. Raymond Veldhuis and Assoc. Prof. Luuk Spreeuwers.
